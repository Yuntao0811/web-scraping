About 701 - 17 Dundonald St

701 - 17 Dundonald St is a Toronto condo which was for rent, near Yonge and Wellesley. It was listed at $1650/mo in April 2021 but is no longer available and has been taken off the market (Leased).. This condo has 1 bed, 1 bathroom and is (over) 499 sqft. Situated in Toronto's Church St. Corridor neighbourhood, Yonge and Bloor , Bay St. Corridor , Yorkville and St. James Town are nearby neighbourhoods.

There are quite a few restaurants to choose from around 17 Dundonald St. Some good places to grab a bite are Avocado Sushi and A&W . Venture a little further for a meal at The Keg Steakhouse + Bar - Mansion , Subway or Pi Co. . If you love coffee, you're not too far from Starbucks located at 8 Wellesley Street E. Groceries can be found at Rabba Fine Foods Stores which is not far and you'll find Rexall Pharma Plus nearby as well. Entertainment around Totem Condos is easy to come by, with Green Space On Church , Imagine Cinemas and Cineplex Cinemas Varsity and VIP only a 4 minute walk. With Toronto Police Museum , Sun Life Financial Museum + Arts Pass and The Gardiner Museum a 5-minute walk from your door, you'll always have something to do on a day off or weekend. Love being outside? Look no further than Norman Jewison Park , Barbara Hall Park or George Hislop Park , which are only steps away from 17 Dundonald St.

If you are looking for transit, don't fear, 17 Dundonald St has a TTC SubwayStop (WELLESLEY STATION - NORTHBOUND PLATFORM) only steps away. It also has (Subway) route 1 Line 1 (yonge university) close by.
